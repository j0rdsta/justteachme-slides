A growing number of people are buying smart phones, and struggling with the basics of how to use it.

Almost 8 in 10 people have a smartphone in Australia
(AIMIA, 2013)

People look for help through:

  • The telco they are with
  • Family - especially the youth
  • Online - but most resources are overly technical

This is Just Teach Me

A learning platform to teach people how to use their smartphones

Suitable for all kinds of cognitive learning styles:

  • Visual & Kinesthetic learners - interactive, animated and engaging screenshots on every step of the lesson
  • Auditory learners - voice-overs
